About the Conference

The IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence (CAI) is a leading event bringing together researchers, industry experts, and practitioners to share advancements in AI. Join us for insightful discussions and groundbreaking innovations.

Explore diverse verticals like AI in healthcare, autonomous systems, natural language processing, and robotics. Sessions will feature expert talks, panels, and interactive workshops.

Participate in hands-on workshops and tutorials led by industry leaders and researchers. Enhance your skills and learn the latest tools in AI development.
